# Brand and image sources

This ledger records the visual sources used by the public website. It is not a
license grant; the project owner remains responsible for retaining the source
licenses and purchase records.

## Official Omala identity

The four outlined SVG files in `public/brand/` were supplied by Omala / Iknite
Studio on 16 July 2026. They contain no font, raster or external-file
dependencies and must not be distorted, rotated, recolored, rearranged or given
a drop shadow.

- `omala-kitchen-lockup-wide.svg`
- `omala-kitchen-lockup-stacked.svg`
- `omala-kitchen-logotype.svg`
- `omala-kitchen-logomark.svg`

The exact logo colors are lime `#8DD122`, orange `#F9A865` and white. Accessible
darker companion colors are used for website text and controls; the official
SVG artwork itself is unchanged.

editor from Adobe Stock 612519432. The approved source remains unchanged.

> Edit this existing licensed stock photograph for Omala Kitchen Hub. Preserve
> the man exactly as photographed: same identity, face, expression, hair, skin
> tone, body, white shirt, hands, pose, phone shape and blank white phone screen,
> pizza slice, camera angle, framing, shadows, and photographic realism. Change
> ONLY the flat yellow studio background into a clean Omala brand studio
> background using the exact primary lime #8DD122 as the dominant field with a
> very subtle soft warm-orange #F9A865 glow toward the far right edge. No words,
> no logo, no icons, no app interface, no additional objects, no patterns, no
> food changes. Keep a crisp professional commercial-photo finish and the
> original landscape aspect ratio.
